## ‚≠ê Main Features

- **Save & Close:** Main feature. Saves all tabs in current window to a list and closes them immediately to free up RAM/CPU.
- **Save Only:** Saves a copy of all open tabs but *does not close them*. Ideal for quick backups.
- **Restore Sessions:** Recover all tabs in a group by clicking "Restore All". Or open individual tabs.
- **Delete Tabs:** To remove a tab without opening it, use the cross icon (√ó) that appears on hover.
- **Smart Search:** Filter instantly by group name, tab title, URL or date. Matches are highlighted.
- **Delete Duplicates:** Remove identical tabs across all groups to save space.
- **Total Privacy:** Your data never leaves your device. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers.

## üñ±Ô∏è Context Menu (Right Click)

Right-click on any page to access AllTab:

- **Send this tab:** Saves only the current tab.
- **Send all except this:** Closes and saves all other tabs, keeping only the current one.
- **Send tabs to the right:** Saves and closes tabs to the right.
- **Send tabs to the left:** Saves and closes tabs to the left.

## üì§ Export & Share

- **Export HTML:** Generates an interactive `.html` file with 7 beautiful themes. Portable and offline.
- **Print / PDF:** Clean view optimized for printing or saving as PDF.
- **Export List (TXT):** Get a plain list of URLs. Useful for sharing or using in other apps.
- **Backup JSON:** Create a full backup of your data to import on another device.
- **Import & Restore:** Restore tabs from JSON. Support for 'Merge' (keep existing) or 'Replace All'.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Configuration

- **Pagination:** Choose how many groups to see per page: 3, 50, 100 or All.
- **URL Visibility:** Toggle between showing full URLs, domains, or hiding them.
- **Delete on open:** Recommended. Deletes link from list when opened to keep list clean.
- **Open in foreground:** If enabled, the browser switches to the new tab immediately. Disable to open in background.
- **Auto-Start:** Automatically opens your dashboard when you launch the browser.
- **Theme:** Toggle between light and dark mode.
